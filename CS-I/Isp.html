What was the concept?

The point of the game is to tell people facts, even simple facts to fun facts. This game contains questions of any topic that you want to play. To win the game, players will have to do their best and pick the right answer to get points. It also academically teach people knowledge that they did not know before and any fun facts that not much people know about. Many people play this game for entertainment and students could even make up their own topic and use this game as a fun quiz to study instead of writing flash cards.
