Symptoms Quiz

By: Tejasvi Gutta, Andrew Nguyen , Shreya Kumar , Melanie Lopez De Nava Reza , and Preston Nguyen.

CS-II Page    Projects Page

Journal

This year, my team decided to make a website called "Symptoms Quiz" for our ISP. It complies most common illnesses and diseases that everyone can get. You just need to put in the information of how you feel into one quiz to see what kind of illness you have. It is going to ask to check your age, gender, and any disorders/diseases you have or others of your family members might be carrying around, which may as well be in your genetics. With thoses informations, you would most likely to get closest and most accurate result.
